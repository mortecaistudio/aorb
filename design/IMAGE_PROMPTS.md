# AORB image-generation prompt set

Built-in image generation was used for all raster concepts and production imagery.

## Shared direction

Monumental European civic futurism; serious and credible architectural editorial photography; phonk album atmosphere; Swiss brutalist typography for concept mockups; void black, cold white, restrained violet `#8B5CF6`, signal green `#B7FF00`, and cyan `#00E5FF`; sharp geometry, subtle grain and scan lines. Avoid hacker hoodies, weapons, riots, destruction, generic neon grids, excessive glow, startup card grids, fake metrics, and watermarks.

## Concepts

1. AORB opening viewport with code-native copy: “EUROPE MUST EVOLVE.”, manifesto text, navigation, and two actions; European civic night architecture on the right and negative space on the left.
2. Manifesto section with the heading “REBELLION BECOMES RECONSTRUCTION.”, asymmetrical editorial layout, rain-soaked European civic plaza, vertical section rail, and closing declaration.
3. Ten-commitment editorial index with huge signal-green numbers, thin rules, alternating rhythm, and an abstract chrome European star-orbit motif; no card grid.
4. Closing sequence with European Parliament image band, four-step method rail, and a monumental white closing panel reading “BUILD THE EUROPE THAT DOES NOT YET EXIST.”

## Production assets

1. Wide nighttime composite European civic cityscape with contemporary parliament architecture, historic civic buildings, river and elevated rail; architecture weighted right with deep-black negative space left; no text or logos.
2. Wide European civic plaza after rain, historic courthouse opposite a contemporary glass institution, with a single violet light monolith suggesting reconstruction; no text or dominant flags.
3. Ultra-wide European Parliament at stormy dusk, low architectural angle with bridges and civic infrastructure; building weighted right and dark open space left; no text or protest imagery.
